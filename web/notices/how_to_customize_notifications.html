You could enjoy custom notifications, for example to receive notifications concerning EFCC do the following:

* Click the menu drawer and a menu will display

* Go to Settings  >  Words to be notified of

* Now enter EFCC

Of course you could enter any other words e.g:

* chelsea fc, all share index, stocks

* kardasian, tiwa, wizkid

*buhari, corruption, efcc

Preference will be given to any words you enter for your notifications. Here's my current personal selection:
