No. 1 Tourist Destination In Nigeria - You Haven't Been To

You probably haven't even heard of Kajuru Castle. Well a picture is worth a thousand words. Some pictures...

According to abujafacts.ng, Kajuru Castle was built in 1978 by a controversial German expatriate who lived in Kaduna at the time. This breathtaking architectural master-piece is a tourist wonder of sorts. The style is very European and clearly German with a baronial hall, complete with suits of armour.

On tripadvisor.com, Kajuru Castle had only 5 reviews (1 very good and 3 excellent). With one reviewer returning after the first visit (Usually a very good sign).

Kajuru Castle is currently not on Google Maps! (Ministry of Tourism!) Well Kajuru Town is located at 10.3225401,7.6815998. Open google map and type in those figures as is and you will be shown the location or simply type Kajuru Kaduna.
